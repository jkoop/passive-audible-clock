Passive Audible Clock

This page will beep at the top of every hour with the format long, short, short, short up to 4 beeps total, at minute 20 with the format short, and at minute 40 with the format short, short.

Due to some security stuff, you have to press this button to start the clock:

Time Beep
0:00, 4:00, 8:00, etc. long, short, short, short
1:00, 5:00, 9:00, etc. long
2:00, 6:00, 10:00, etc. long, short
3:00, 7:00, 11:00, etc. long, short, short
x:20 short
x:40 short, short
